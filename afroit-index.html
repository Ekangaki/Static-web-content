About AIT

Afro IT Solutions (AIT) is a world-class institution of higher learning located in Nguti, Cameroon. Established in 2016, AIT has a strong tradition of academic excellence and a commitment to preparing students for successful careers in a variety of fields. Our faculty are experts in their fields and are dedicated to providing students with a well-rounded education and the skills they need to succeed in their careers.

Mission: To provide accessible, high-quality technology education to empower individuals and communities.

Faculty

At AIT, we are proud of the talented and dedicated faculty who make our institution a leader in higher education. Our faculty members are experts in their fields and are committed to providing students with a top-quality education.
